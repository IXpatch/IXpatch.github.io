Habipets is a habit-forming pet simulation game for Android. Your pets and plants grow as you accomplish your real-life goals, and keep you motivated by rewarding your personal progress. It's written in C# using Unity Engine.

PORTALGOLF

Based on the 2007 physics puzzle hit game Portal, this minigolf simulator incorporates unusual elements like portals and propulsion gel to mix up the usual golf experience. This game was written from scratch in Java, without using any pre-made physics libraries or engines, in conjunction with a small group of friends.
